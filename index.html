About me

Benoît Legat is a postdoctoral researcher at KU Leuven with Prof. Bart De Moor in STADIUS. He received his Ph.D. degree in applied mathematics from the UCLouvain, Belgium, in 2020. His research interests include mathematical optimization with a focus on polynomial optimization, invariant set computation and optimal control.
